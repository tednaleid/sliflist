Hung Jury (Adept)

For complete perks, see d2gunsmith.com, light.gg or destinytracker.com.

The Adept Hung Jury is available from Grandmaster Nightfalls. It’s the same as the Hung Jury, with the exception that it can take Adept mods and gains +2 to all attributes when Masterworked.

PvP Thoughts#

Scout Rifles in PvP still aren’t shining but there are some really interesting rolls here that you can bolster with insane Adept mods. For example, if you slot an Adept Counterbalance mod, you’ll bring the Recoil to 100. Unfortunately that hurts your effective Range taking you from 83.36m to 80.05m ğŸ™ƒ
