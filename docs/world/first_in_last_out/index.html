[First In, Last Out](https://www.light.gg/db/items/3616586446)

“First In, Last Out” was released for the first time in Season 11 since its initial drop at the launch of Destiny 2 - Season 1! It’s the first time we’ve seen this with random rolls, so it does have a contemporary set of perks available (e.g. Demoiltionist and Vorpal). With the S14 shotgun nerf, and the mid-2020 precision damage PvE buff for Shotguns, (quoted below) this one should be at the top of your list.

PvP Thoughts#

Slug shotguns have always been an acquired taste and nothing about the First In, Last Out changes that. It can do great work in the right hands. Grab some Range- and Accuracy-boosting perks and you’ll be all set.

PvE Thoughts#

In the 5/21/2020 TWAB, Bungie announced a surprising buff to slug shotguns:

Slug Shotguns are underused in PvE, because they require crits to maximize damage, and didn’t reward that precision with higher damage per second: Slug Shotgun PvE Damage + 30%.

This rocketed slugs to the top of the boss DPS leaderboards. Definitely pick one up.

🕺 Aztecross' “Hip Shooter” (pvp)#

To quote my friend Lenny, “This is the kind of stupid idea I am fond of.” Aztec’s contention is that slug shotguns struggle because of high enemy mobility combined with having to zoom means a much tighter field of view that makes them even harder to track. This is precisely why I stop using Chaperone around 4000 Glory in Comp. Hunters with high mobility (the stat and the skill to do so) and charging shotgun rushers become nearly impossible to hit (for me at least!). This is a series of rolls based around Hip-Fire Grip and Opening Shot. It’s a totally different way of playing and I love the idea of it! I doubt it will become meta, but this list is all about fun, specific rolls so here you go 😃

Collector’s Edition Roll

  • Barrels: Full Bore
  • Magazines: Accurized Rounds, Assault Mag
  • Perks 1: Hip-Fire Grip
  • Perks 2: Opening Shot
  • Masterworks: Range MW

Extended Perks (referred to with a ‘+’ below)

  • Barrels: Full Bore, Hammer-Forged Rifling, Extended Barrel, Smallbore, Corkscrew Rifling
  • Magazines: Accurized Rounds, Light Mag, Assault Mag
