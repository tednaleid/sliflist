Compass Rose

For complete perks, see d2gunsmith.com, light.gg or destinytracker.com.

Compass Rose is a Precision Frame, Solar shotgun. It was available only during the Season 14 Solstice event.

🧲 Magnetic North (pvp)#

Classic “SnapDraw” shotgun roll. Compass Rose can roll with a few Range- increasing barrels in addition to just Full Choke and Rifled, so our odds of picking up these rolls is a bit better.

Collector’s Edition Roll

  • Barrels: Rifled Barrel, Full Choke
  • Magazines: Accurized Rounds
  • Perks 1: Quickdraw
  • Perks 2: Snapshot
  • Masterworks: Range MW

Extended Perks (referred to with a ‘+’ below)

  • Barrels: Rifled Barrel, Full Choke, Smallbore, Corkscrew Rifling
  • Magazines: Accurized Rounds, Light Mag, Assault Mag

🧭 Directional Insanity (pve)#

This is for champions or bosses. With Surplus or Field Prep, you’ll be able to make the most out of Vorpal. Currently with the damage bonus slugs received a few seasons back, this is still beat out by FILO, but it’s a solid alternative if you pick one up.

Collector’s Edition Roll

  • Barrels: Rifled Barrel, Full Choke
  • Magazines: Assault Mag
  • Perks 1: Surplus, Field Prep
  • Perks 2: Vorpal
  • Masterworks: Range MW

Extended Perks (referred to with a ‘+’ below)

🌠 North Star (pve)#

Surplus and Wellspring are just so perfectly suited for one another that I’m compelled to create rolls that capture them when available. With two abilities down, I was able to get both of them back after five or six red bar kills. This would be a good workhorse roll, suitable if you feel like bringing a shotgun into many of the VoG or DSC encounters.

Collector’s Edition Roll

  • Barrels: Rifled Barrel, Full Choke
  • Magazines: Assault Mag
  • Perks 1: Surplus
  • Perks 2: Wellspring
  • Masterworks: Range MW

Extended Perks (referred to with a ‘+’ below)

  • Barrels: Rifled Barrel, Full Choke, Smallbore, Corkscrew Rifling
