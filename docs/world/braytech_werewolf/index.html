BrayTech Werewolf

The BrayTech Werewolf is a 450 RPM “Precision Frame” Kinetic Auto Rifle first introduced in Season 8. It’s now available in the 2020 Festival of the Lost event, with random rolls. The only competitive AR archetype right now are the 600s, so you’re gambling on an eventual sandbox change for this to be more effective in PvP.
