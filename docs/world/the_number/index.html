The Number

The Number is a 450 RPM Precision Frame, Arc Energy Auto Rifle, offered as a random roll beginning in Season 14. It initially appeared years ago in the “Faction Rallies” events as part of the Future War Cult weapon pool. It compares closely to the Shadow Price, a Nightfall-only reward offerd in Season 13.
