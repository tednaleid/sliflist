[Vision of Confluence (Timelost)](https://www.light.gg/db/items/690668916) lgg

This is the “Timelost” (or “Adept”) version of this weapon, which drops only by completing the Conflux encounter Challenge in Vault of Glass on Master mode.

The rolls here are the same as the normal version except with a higher drop chance because the Timelost version comes with double perks in the final two columns.
